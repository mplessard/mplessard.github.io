Easy roasted eggplant recipe

Thu 20 August 2020      Marie

It's eggplant season! 🍆 I rarely cook eggplant, I don't remember having eaten eggplant with my parents. It's a vegetable that I don't think is very popular. As I received this vegetable in my SecondLife basket, I had to brainstorm and find a recipe that was going to please me.

I finally decided to roast the eggplants in the oven and eat them in a buddha bowl. 🥗 I liked it so much that I ate it all week long!

For 4 portions

Ingredients

  • 1 large eggplant
  • 2 tablespoon of salt
  • 2 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of maple syrup
  • 1 tablespoon of lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 2 tablespoon of nutritional yeast (nooch)

Preparation

  1. Cut the eggplant into 1/3 inch thick slices and place them side by side on a clean tea towel.
  2. Sprinkle salt on each side of the eggplant slices.
  3. Leave the eggplant to disgorged of its water for 15-30 minutes.
  4. Meanwhile in a large bowl put the soy sauce, maple syrup, lemon juice, olive oil and nutritional yeast and mix everything together.
  5. After 15-30 minutes, wipe off the water and some of the salt from the eggplant with a clean tea towel.
  6. Preheat the oven to 350 °F.
  7. Cut the eggplant slices into strips. (optional)
  8. Line a baking sheet with a silicone sheet or parchment paper.
  9. Place the eggplant strips on the baking sheet.
  10. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until the eggplant is roasted.
