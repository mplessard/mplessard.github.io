Easy ecofriendly all-purpose cleaner

Sun 09 August 2020      Marie

Do you know how easy it is to make your own homemade all-purpose cleaner? In addition to being an ecological alternative, it is also an economical alternative without risk to health. ðŸ™‚

You'll need

  • a container
  • Citrus peels
  • vinegar
  • a vaporizer
  • water
  • a label maker (optional)

Instructions

  1. Place the citrus peels in the container.
  2. Pour the vinegar up to the rim of the container.
  3. Let the citrus infuse in the vinegar for at least 2 weeks.
  4. After two weeks, remove the citrus peels from the vinegar.
  5. Pour with a 1:1 ratio, water and citrus infused vinegar into the spray.

There you go, you are now ready to clean your home in an ecological way! ðŸ™‚
