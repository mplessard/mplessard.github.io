The best orchard salsa

Sun 02 August 2020      Marie

Not being a tomato fan, salsas never appealed to me. But for the past few years, I can't get enough of eating this green apple salsa. It often serves me as a late afternoon snack or appetizer when I people come over. It also happens that I prepare it to accompany tacos. It has become a staple in my kitchen!

This recipe is inspired by a recipe I tasted during a Tupperware party organized by my friend Stéphanie. Since the recipe was not vegan I adapted this one to suit my diet, my tastes, but especially what I have in my pantry.

Ingredients

  • 3 green apples
  • 1/2 red pepper
  • 1-2 French shallots
  • 1 jalapeno pepper (quantity to taste)
  • 1 lime
  • 1 tablespoon of maple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon of chipotle pepper powder

Preparation

  1. Remove the peel from the shallots.
  2. Cut the apples and peppers into large wedges.
  3. In a food processor, finely chopped the French shallots and the jalapeno pepper.
  4. Add the apples and peppers in small quantities to the food processor to roughly chop them.
  5. Add the lime juice, the tablespoon of maple syrup and the chipotle pepper powder.
  6. Mix everything in the food processor and serve.
