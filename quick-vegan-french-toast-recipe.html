Quick vegan french toast recipe

Dim 26 juillet 2020      Marie

Have you ever think about what you could do with some dry out bread from the day before? Well I did, and I creater a recipe to reduce waste! Instead of composting your dry bread, I invite you to make this vegan french toast recipe.ðŸ˜‰

4 portions

Ingredients

  • 1 cup plant-based milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon of plant-based margarine
  • 8 bread slices of about 1/2 inch think
  • 1 1/2 cup of sliced strawberries
  • maple sirup
  • vanilla plant-based yogourt
  • 1/4 cup sliced almonds

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix the milk, cinnamon, salt and vanilla extract.
  2. Place the bread slices in the mixture and let them soak in. Let the excess drain.
  3. In a frying pan, heat 1/4 tablespoon of margarine over medium heat.
  4. Place the slices of bread in the pan and cook them for about 3 minutes per side or until golden brown. Repeat step 3 and 4 with the other slices.
  5. When ready to serve, garnish the French toast with strawberries, maple syrup, yogourt, and sliced almonds.
