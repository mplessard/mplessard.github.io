Summer fruit salad

Sat 29 August 2020      Marie

When life offers you fruits, make a salad üôÇ üçèüçëü•ù This salad is as good as a snack or a dessert. For a decadent dessert option, add a splash of chocolate and your sweet tooth will be sated.

4 small portions or 2 large

Ingredients

  • 2 kiwis
  • 2 peaches
  • 2 green apples
  • 1 lime
  • 1/4 teaspoon of vanilla
  • 1 teaspoon of maple syrup

Preparation

  1. Cut all the fruit into cubes and put them in a bowl.
  2. Add lime zest and juice, vanilla and maple syrup.
  3. Mix everything together and serve.
